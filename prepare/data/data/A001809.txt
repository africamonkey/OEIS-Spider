[Stern, Terquem] Proof: For fixed i,j and for fixed I,J (i<j, I>J, 1 <= i,j,I,J <= n), we have (n-2)! permutations p of [n] for which p(i)=I and p(j)=J (permute {1,2,...,n} \\ {I,J} in the positions (1,2,...,n) \\ {i,j}).
