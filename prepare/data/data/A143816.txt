Then the real number R(n) is an integral linear combination of R(0) = 1 + 1/3! + 1/6! + ...., R(2) - R(1) = 1/1! + 1/4! + 1/7! + ... and R(1) = 1/2! + 1/5! + 1/8! + ... .
