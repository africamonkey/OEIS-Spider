    "comment": [
        "Given n, an auxiliary sequence B_n(m) is defined by B_n(m) = A000045(m), 0<=m<3 and B_n(m)=round(x_n*B_n(m-1)), m>=3, where x_n is a truncated approximation of the golden ratio A001622 = 1.61803398..., namely, x_n = floor(A001622*10^n)/10^n =1, 1.6, 1.61, 1.618,... If one would replace x_n by the exact value of golden ratio, the B_n(m) would reproduce the Fibonacci sequence. The sequence shows the index where B_n(m) diverges first from Fibonacci(m): B_n(m) = Fibonacci(m) for 0<=m<a(n) and B_n(m)< Fibonacci(m) for m=a(n)."
