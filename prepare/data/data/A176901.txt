    "comment": [
        "A Latin rectangle is called reduced if its first row is [1,2,...,n] (the number of 3Xn reduced Latin rectangles is given in A000186). Therefore a Latin rectangle having exactly n fixed points in the first two rows may be called \"semireduced\". Thus if A1(i), A2(i), i=1,...,n, are two first rows, then, for every i, either A1(i)=i or A2(i)=i."
