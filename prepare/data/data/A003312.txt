        "This sequence was originally defined in Popular Computing in 1974 by a sieve, as follows. Write down the numbers from 3 to infinity. Take next number, M say, that has not been crossed off. The numbers that are left form the sequence. The recurrence given here for the sequence was found by _Colin Mallows_. The problem asked for the 1000-th term, and was unsolved for several years."
