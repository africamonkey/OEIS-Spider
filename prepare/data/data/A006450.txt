        "Lim_{n->infinity}a(n)/(n*(log(n))^2) = 1. Proof: By Cipolla's asymptotic formula, prime(n) ~ L(n) + R(n), where L(n)/n = log(n) + log(log(n)) - 1 and R(n)/n decreases logarithmically to 0.
